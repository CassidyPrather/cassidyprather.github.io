A **webring** is an old-web way to wander: a loop of sites that each link to the
next, so a visitor can hop around the whole circle. **UDURGH** is one such loop.
The widget below gives every member matching **‹ prev · random · next ›**
controls, all pointing back into the ring.

It's a single iframe served from this site, so there's nothing to install and
nothing to keep in sync — add a member here and every embed updates itself.
