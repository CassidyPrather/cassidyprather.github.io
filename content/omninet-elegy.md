<h1>Утопия: Omninet Elegy</h1>

<div class="quote">
  <p>Silence...</p>

  <p>Characters that survived <a href="../arms-derby/">Armament Derby</a> are licensed for the acausal technology they escaped with. No character remembers the events of Armament Derby.</p>
</div>

<hr/>

<p>Утопия is <a href="https://massifpress.com/lancer">Lancer</a>, except:</p>
<ul>
  <li>
    New round structure:
    <ol>
      <li>Characters declare actions and moves simultaneously. <em>Write it down. You can't change your mind.</em></li>
      <li>PC actions resolve.</li>
      <li>NPC actions resolve.</li>
      <li>PC moves resolve.</li>
      <li>NPC moves resolve.</li>
    </ol>
  </li>
  <li>A character's action and move phases are not different turns.</li>
  <li>When a unit becomes engaged, its staged move is cancelled.</li>
  <li>PC Mechs survive 0- structure. <em>Continue to roll the structure damage table as usual.</em></li>
  <li>PC Mechs survive 0- stress. <em>Continue to roll the overheating table as usual.</em></li>
</ul>
