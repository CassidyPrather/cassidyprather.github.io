There's a blog here now. Long posts get collapsed to a summary on the feed,
with the full text a click away; short posts show up on the feed whole.

<!--more-->

A few notes on how this place works, mostly so future-me remembers:

- Every post has a timestamp and a permalink — the `#` at the bottom of each
  entry on the feed.
- Posts marked as highlights show up in the Highlights column beside the
  feed. This post is one, so the column isn't empty on day one.
- The search box searches the full text of every post, not just what's
  visible on the feed.

That's it. More soon, probably.
